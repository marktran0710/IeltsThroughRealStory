Reading scene

At 8:20 a.m., Linh realised her backpack was no longer beside her seat. She walked to the information desk, where a staff member asked for the train number, the colour of the bag, and a list of important items inside. Linh felt nervous, but she gave short, accurate details and received a reference code.

The staff member explained that lost property reports are usually checked within forty-eight hours. If the bag is found, Linh must bring photo ID and describe one item that is not visible from outside the bag.

Station announcement

"Passengers who left personal items on the Green Line should visit Counter 3 before 6 p.m. Please prepare a description of the item and your journey details."

Part 2 cue card

Describe a time you lost something important.

You should say what you lost, where it happened, what you did next, and explain how you felt afterwards.

01:00

Use the minute to collect three verbs, two feelings, and one result.

Writing Task 1: request email

Write to the lost property office. Explain what happened, describe the backpack, and ask what you should do next.

  • Open with the purpose of your email.
  • Give exact details: time, line, colour, items.
  • End with a polite request and contact details.
